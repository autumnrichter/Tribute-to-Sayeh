Sayeh

Date Purchased from Breeder: 9/24/2009

This is a fun tribute page to our loyal companions!

Sayeh is a purebred Doberman who has very human-like mannerisms and expressions. She an extremely special dog for so many reasons, one of which is that she is a service dog for people with Post Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD) and anxiety disorders. She has an amazing backstory which is one of the many reasons why she is such an excellent service dog and companion.

  1. Her name is means “shadow” and is of Persian/Iranian origin. She got this name because she used to always follow her owner around like a shadow.
  2. She Loves Nori Sheets
  3. She Loves the Honest Kitchen Ocean Chews Crispy Cod Fish Skins
  4. She Loves to Accessorize (Scarves, Pearl Necklaces, etc.
  5. She is a Great Snuggler
  6. She can do a fun trick that consists of a “Sit, Down, and Up” command just by following only hand signals (no voice commands) and doing all sequentially before getting a treat.
  7. She has excellent manners and is very attentive and intelligent: She walks well on a leash without pulling and is well trained to follow commands immediately.
  8. She has incredible powers of reducing anxiety, even when you don’t realize how anxious you actually are.
  9. She has titanium rods and screws in both her front legs.
  10. She loves going to the dog park and playing with her dog pals.
  11. She can be flirtatious with certain male dogs.
  12. She loves puppies.
  13. She loves the birthday parties her mother throws for her and really enjoys opening all of her birthday presents.
  14. I love this dog as if she were my own and have known and helped take care of her for 10 of her 14 years of life so far...I’m basically her second mommy
